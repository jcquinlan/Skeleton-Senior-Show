about us!

Every year in Richmond, VA the graduating class of the Photography and Film Department of VCUarts puts on a senior show open to the public as a celebration of our talent and education. Every student in the department is interesed in vastly different approaches to photographic and filmic art-making and it is our hope that this final showcase of our work will allow the public to glimpse what we have been so arduously refininf in our studios over the last three years.

Being studious artists and hard workers we plan on putting on the absolute most engaging show possible, but we need to turn to the public to aid us in funding and supporting what will hopefully be a vibrant example of the RVA art scene and community. Please read about your role in making this show a possibility, and make sure to follow us on our various social media and donate on our GoFundMe page.
