about us!

Every year in Richmond, VA the graduating class of the Photography and Film Department of VCUarts puts on a senior show open to the public as a celebration of our talent and education. Every student in the department is interesed in vastly different approaches to photographic and filmic art-making and it is our hope that this final showcase of our work will allow the public to see what we have been so arduously refining in our studios over the last three years.

Being dedicated artists and hard workers we plan on putting on the most engaging show possible, but we need to turn to the public to aid us in funding and supporting what will hopefully be a vibrant example of the RVA art scene. Please read about your role in making this show a possibility, follow us on our various social media, and donate on our GoFundMe page.

how you can help!

In order for this show to become the monumental success we are hoping for, we will need to rely on the generosity and support of the public. While we have numerous fundraising opportunities planned, it is unlikely that these events alone will provide us with the amount of money necessary to put on a professional and expansive show. We encourage you to donate what you can and leave a supportive message on our GoFundMe page linked below.
